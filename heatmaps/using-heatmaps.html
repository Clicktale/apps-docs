Using Heatmaps

Using Heatmaps

After you finish setting up your application for heat-maps, to access the heat map, navigate to the heat maps area of Clicktale for Apps.

Segmentation of heatmaps is a little different than the rest of the product. They are always segmented by the platform, ‘Page View’ and the application version – before applying any other segmentation filters familiar from the session playback or other features available in Clicktale for Apps.

We segment according to Page View because taps data is only relevant to the page displayed right now. This logical page or screen is may differ from one version to another as the UX or design may change and of course, things may not look the same between different platforms.

These reasons are why the segmentation is done first by the Platform, Page and Version, and only then the rest of the segmentation filters are applied such as events, users and so on.

The heat map displayed is using only the tap data and can be referred to as a tap heat map, no swipes or pinch gestures are included in the heat map.
